Frequently Asked Questions

Who must have a PAN?

The PAN is mandatory for all existing taxpayers and persons for filing of income tax returns, and for all correspondences with the income tax authorities. The PAN is also required to be quoted by persons entering into financial transactions that are notified by the Central Board of Direct Taxes.

How do I get a PAN application form?

Please download the online application form from the Alankit website

What are the document requirements for a PAN application?

The following documents are required to be submitted with the application form:

  • A] Proof of Identity (POI)
  • B] Proof of Address (POA)
  • C] Proof of Date of Birth (PODB)

Note: PODB is applicable basis the individual and HUF status of the applicant. The POI and POA documents are applicable basis the citizenship status of the applicant.

For proofs of POI, POA and PODB, copies of any of the following documents are eligible:

  • - School Leaving Certificate
  • - Matriculation Certificate
  • - Degree Certificate from a recognized educational institution
  • - Depository or Bank Account
  • - Water Bill
  • - Ration Card
  • - Property Tax Assessment Order
  • - Passport
  • - Voter Identity Card
  • - Driving License
  • - Identity Certificate signed by a MP, MLA, Municipal Councilor or Gazetted Officer

If the PAN applicant is a minor, any of the above stated documents of the parent or guardian of the minor will serve as POI.

If a PAN application is made on behalf of a HUF, any of the above stated documents of the Karta of the HUF will serve as POI.
